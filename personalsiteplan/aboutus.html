WHY AND HOW DO WE DO THIS?

Huswell Trading are owned by Mr. Marvin S. Tavarez and his wife, Mrs. Dianne Huswell C. Tavarez. It was started since 1994. It all starts to a hobby that turned into a family business which immediately gets more popular in the province of Laguna. Laguna was known as a place where there's a lot of what they called "magtataka" or box/paper mache makers. Huswell Trading was stopped due to family and personal reasons and challenges. Now, in 2014, Huswell Trading rise up again and it was now a registered Small Business Enterprise in the BIR (Bureau of Internal Revenue), DTI (Department of Trade and Industry), and others registrations locally.

Huswell Trading nowadays is serving a lot of well-renowned companies and big clients like Kumu Philippines, Coca-Cola, OKADA Manila, Solaire Philippines, Tessera and Chemisol, Flowerstore PH, and many more. We are now getting bigger in serving and providing high-quality packaging needs like Hard Rigid Boxes, Corporate Boxes, PR Boxes, Offset Boxes, and a lot of Packaging Needs and Services.

Huswell Trading believes in the saying "if ye are in the service of your fellow beings, ye are only in the service of your God." Because of that saying, we are trying our best everyday to serve all our clients with optimism and enthusiasm to provide every needful service we can.
